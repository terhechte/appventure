What makes the iPad 2 and keyboard combo so effective for my writing is the “one app at a time” nature of the tablet. The running app takes up the entire screen, and thus my focus. There are no distractions presented while writing, just inputting words on the screen. This is liberating for a writer, and I find I can write more, and better, on the tablet system than on a “real” computer.
